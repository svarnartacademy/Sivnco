I'm Shiv — H P Shivaraj. I lead brand and communication design at Jus Amazin, teach art at Openhouse learning hubs, and co-run Svarnart Academy with my wife. Born and raised in Namma Bengaluru, that texture shows up in everything I make.

Design is how I think. Art is how I feel. Both live in everything I put out — whether it's a packaging system for a D2C brand or an acrylic painting on a Saturday afternoon.
