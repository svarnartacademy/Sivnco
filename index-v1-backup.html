About the person
More than
a designer.

I'm Shiv — and I refuse to be one thing. During the day I lead brand and communication design at Jus Amazin, and teach art at Openhouse. I co-run Svarnart Academy with my wife. Through Sivnco I take on studio work for brands that care about craft.

But beyond the professional — I'm a painter who believes colour is a language. A writer who thinks clearly by writing first. A craftsman who believes the details are the work. I was born and raised in Namma Bengaluru, and that texture shows up in everything I make.
